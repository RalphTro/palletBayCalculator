Pallet Bay Calculator

a simple heuristic for calculating the number of required pallet bays for an order of trade items

Preliminary notes

  • This heuristic was developed as a basis for optimising the capacity utilisation of trucks.
  • The procedure is deliberately kept simple, thus does not yield exact outputs.
  • At the same time, it was validated by industry and considered to be sufficiently viable.
  • In EDI, indicating the required number of pallet bays may be relevant in ORDERS, IFTMIN, and DESADV.
